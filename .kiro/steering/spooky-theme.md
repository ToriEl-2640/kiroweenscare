# Spooky Theme Steering

## Tone & Voice
- Use Halloween/horror-themed language throughout
- Maintain a playful, not genuinely scary tone
- Reference monsters, haunting, curses, and supernatural elements
- Keep it fun and engaging, not dark or disturbing

## Color Palette
- Background: Dark grays, blacks, deep purples
- Accents: Blood red (#dc2626), toxic green, eerie purple
- Text: Light grays, whites for contrast
- Severity colors:
  - Critical: Red
  - High: Orange
  - Medium: Yellow
  - Low: Blue

## Animation Style
- Flickering text (like candles)
- Floating elements (ghostly)
- Fog/mist effects
- Subtle hover animations
- No jump scares or aggressive animations
